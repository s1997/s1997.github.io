More indulge your senses in the world's most romantic place Our Lights What you can expect in Portofino Portofino is a fantastic location, based in the Riviera Ligure, Italy, considered by many, one of the world's eight wonders. It is a small town of no more than 500 people, placed upon its bay. It's kind of a natural bowl which has become the world's famous Piazzetta.

Portofino became famous in the 60s, when the rich and famous characters of the "Dolce Vita", actors, actresses, pop stars, and rich aristocrats frequently started visiting it for its twilights, exclusive parties, and breathtaking views. At the time, the high society moved from Via Veneto and Piazza di Spagna in Rome during weekends to Portofino, Capri, Saint Tropez and Monaco, Cortina and Saint Moritz. These were the compulsory spots, in addition to Porto Cervo, once known as the Costa Smeralda, during summers. Through time, Portofino has seen a high standard of living thanks to its exclusive guests, huge yachts, famous boat races such as the Zegna Trophy and for its incredible and natural beauty.
